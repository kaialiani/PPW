#HEX app

The #Hex application allows you to capture any color of your surroundings and apply it to t-shirts. The idea is to playfully guide the user through the creation process, without getting them bored or frustrated along the way.

My role in this group project was to design the visuals of the app, develop the prototype, and create the mockups.
