FooFest is a festival that offers a magical escape from the ordinary, where attendees can immerse themselves in a sensory journey within a captivating forest setting by the lake, in the Faroe Islands.

This project was built using Next.JS during 3rd semester at KEA.
The goal was to develop a beautiful and functional website for a festival.
I took a key role in the processes of ideation, wireframing, prototyping and developing the user flow and purchase flow, creating an intuitive and responsive digital experience.
